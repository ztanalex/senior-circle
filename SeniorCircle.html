About Our Circle

Our Mission

For over 3 years, Senior Circle has been dedicated to enhancing the lives of seniors in our community. We provide a welcoming space where older adults can socialize, learn, and participate in meaningful activities.

Our center offers a variety of programs including technology training, fitness classes, art workshops, and social events - all designed to keep seniors connected and engaged.

A key mission of Senior Circle is to bring technology access and social fairness to senior people, ensuring everyone can participate fully in our digital world regardless of their background or technical experience.

We believe that every senior deserves to live with dignity, purpose, and connection to their community.

Become a Volunteer

Make a Difference Today

Our volunteers are the heart of Senior Circle. Whether you can spare a few hours a week or just help with special events, your time makes a real difference in the lives of seniors.

We offer flexible volunteering opportunities that match your skills and interests:

  • Event assistance and planning
  • Teaching classes or workshops
  • Companionship and friendly visits
  • Administrative support
  • Technology assistance

No special skills required - just a caring heart and willingness to help!
